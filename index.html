Privacy Policy

Numbry Toolbox

Last updated: 2026-01-17 Applies to: Numbry Toolbox for Android

This policy explains how Numbry Toolbox handles your information. The app is designed to work offline and does not collect personal data.

Quick Summary

Data collection No personal data is collected.
Data sharing No data is shared with third parties.
Permissions No sensitive permissions are requested.

Information We Collect

Numbry Toolbox does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The app does not require an account, and no analytics or advertising SDKs are used.

If you create notes, the content is stored locally on your device in app-private storage. We do not have access to this data.

How We Use Information

  • All calculations and conversions run locally on your device.
  • Notes are used only inside the app and never leave your device.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties and we do not use third-party analytics or ad networks. The app does not connect to external servers.

Data Retention and Deletion

Notes remain on your device until you delete them in the app. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

Children's Privacy

Numbry Toolbox does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at holyeyeking@gmail.com. If you email us, we only use your email address to reply to your request.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect app changes or legal requirements. The date at the top will be updated when changes are made.

Thank you for using Numbry Toolbox.
